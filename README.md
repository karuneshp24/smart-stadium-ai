# Smart Stadium AI Solution

## Overview
A Generative AI–enabled solution designed to optimize stadium operations and enhance the overall tournament experience during the FIFA World Cup 2026.  

This project leverages AI to support:
- Navigation
- Crowd management
- Accessibility
- Transportation
- Sustainability
- Multilingual assistance
- Operational intelligence
- Real-time decision support

## Features
- AI-powered fan navigation and multilingual support
- Dynamic crowd flow management
- Volunteer and staff assistance via AI Copilot
- Real-time operational dashboards
- Sustainable energy and waste optimization
